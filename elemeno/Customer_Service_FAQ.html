Q: Can I make custom orders?
A: Yes, if you contact me and let me know how just what you want...I will let you know if it is possible; this includes me using printed fabric on both sides of the pillow cover.

I can make pillow covers in practically any size and with any fabric (even fabric that you provide).


Q: What type of pillow form should I use?
A: I recommend down or down-alternative pillow forms such as the ones offered at Fabric.com or Joann.com. I have posted the direct links below.

Pillows on Fabric.com
Pillows on Joann.com


Q: What size pillow cover should I purchase?
A: You should purchase a 20" pillow cover for a 20" pillow, an 18" pillow cover for an 18" pillow, etc. This is because I make them slightly smaller than the size mentioned in my descriptions for a pretty and plump look.


Q: How long will it take to ship?
A: As of now, I can quarentee that your order will be processed, made, and shipped within 7 days of the purchase. It is then shipped with USPS First Class or Priority (for larger orders) and once the order ships, an e-mail will be sent to you with a tracking number (for orders within the United States).


Q: Can I have rush delivery?
A: Yes, contact me if you need the order rushed and we can make that happen.


Q: What are the care instructions for the pillow covers?
A: Spot clean or dry clean only. I use high quality home decor fabric so I recommend that you do not wash them in a washing machine.


Q: Can I get a swatch of the fabric first?
A: Yes, just contact me with your address and let me know specifically which swatches you would like! I can send you as many as you need to help you decide which pattern is best.


Q: Can I order cut yardage of your fabric?
A: Yes, contact me and let me know just how much fabric you will need! The cost will depend on how much fabric is needed and the shipping costs.


Q: Can I make something out of my own fabric?
A: Yes, I currently make custom pillow covers with any fabric. Contact me and I'll let you know how much fabric I need and how much it will cost.


Q: Can I get a wholesale discount?
A: Please contact me about larger orders and I will let you know if we will be able to work out a deal.
