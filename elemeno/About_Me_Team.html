Everybit helps...

Ever since ElemenOPillows began, I have been enlisting the help of my three sons Justin, Jon and Jake along with Jon's girlfriend Kristen. It's incredible how much faster the process becomes when fabric is pre-cut and sized out by my helpers! They have made my life a little easier by cutting fabric, running errands and sending out orders when they have free time.

All three of my boys go to (or went to in Justin's case) The Ohio State University. Justin now works in South Carolina at Nestle! Jon is interning at Netjets in Columbus and Jake will be interning at Gulfstream in the summer. Kristen is a junior at Otterbein University here in Westerville and works as an accountant for Otterbein!

Recently, my mother Nancy has been helping me out sewing some of the pillow cases. It's so incredibly helpful and makes working fun!
