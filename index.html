My name is Jake Larkin.

I am currently a graduate student studying aerospace engineering at Ohio State working with Prof. McNamara in the Computational Aero Elasticity Lab (Hypersonics). When I'm not in the lab, you can catch me golfing, playing tennis, or going for a dive (at least while I'm in Florida..)

Westerville Central High School

  • Westerville, Ohio
  • Class of 2010

As a high school student, I developed a handful of passions that I still pursue. I studied physics, chemistry, math and science heavily, but had a sharp interest in computers, technology and the ever expanding internet. I began exploring photography, graphic design outside of the classroom after buying my first DSLR (My trusty Canon).

Outside of the classroom, I spent a majority of my days outdoors goofing off like all teenagers, but I also played a bit of golf and tennis for the school. We weren't particularly good at either, but hey...it's high school.

Once I turned 16, I got a job as a bag boy at a golf course nearby, but my weekend gig was installing sound systems and other electronics in friend's cars. As a senior, I made my first website (ahhh it was so pretty ~ cough cough) for a game we lovingly reffer to as Senior Tag. Basically, it's a glorified game of tag using squirt guns across the whole city...but as the organizer, I had to build a tool that teams could add team members, post updates, and see who had won each round. What a memory.
