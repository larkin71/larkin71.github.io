The Business Builders Club

President

2014-2015 Academic Year

The Business Builders Club is more than a student organization. We are a group of passionate students who collaborate with the Columbus Community and organizations around the world to foster entrepreneurship. The BBC recruits, educates, and funds talented students at Ohio State. Our members are excited by the challenges and opportunities provided by entrepreneurship. Some of us are determined to end poverty. Some of us want to be millionaires. All of us want to change the world.

My involvement with the Business Builders Club began when I was a curious sophomore exploring several sudent organizations that I thought would fit my interests. From day one, I feel in love with the crowd I was surrounded by and the energy present in the room. This year, I will lead a team of amazingly bright and motivated students to change how Ohio State sees and utilizes entrepreneurship. Big things on the horizon.

Inspire OSU

Co-President / Co-Founder

Inspire OSU strives to expand the importance of community service across The Ohio State University. Metro Engineering Club

Founder and Director

2013-2015 Academic Years

The Metro Engineering Club is a STEM outreach program that was designed to expose students at Metro Middle School to STEM related topics and allow them to explore the vast world of engineering. For 10 weeks, engineering students from Ohio State mentored students and guided them through a curriculum based on the principles of circuitry and programming by utilizing the Arudino micro-controller.

The Metro Engineering Club was an idea dreamed up by Dr. Lisa Abrams and I and sponsored by the Honda Partnership at OSU. I have always had a passion for education and youth outreach and this was the perfect opportunity to combine the two. It has been both an exciting and rewarding adventure and I am excited to see where it goes next year!

Common Cents Investment Group

President / Sr. Advisor

2012-2013 Academic Year

Common Cents Investment Group is a student organization open to all students who are interested in learning about personal investments. The focus of the club is to increase the knowledge of our members through the discussion of investing strategies and ideas. We talk about all aspects of investing, including mutual funds, the stock market, options, and many other scenarios, and teach our members the fundamentals of investing. There is something to be learned at every meeting for both beginners and sophisticated investors.

My involvement with CCIG began as a freshman and I became the president my junior year. It was an exciting opportuity to immerse myself into the world of personal finance without any formal education on the topic. I thought I was in a bit over my head, but after studying a handful of books and spending hours reading Investopedia, I began to enjoy bringing CCIG to a new level and helping others learn the fine art of personal investing.

Aerospace Research Associate

A joint opportunity through the Universities Space Research Association (USRA), The University of Florida and the Air Force Research Laboratory (AFRL) to spend the summer on Eglin Air Force Base in Florida.
