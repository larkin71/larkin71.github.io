My name is Jake Larkin.

I am currently a graduate student studying aerospace engineering at Ohio State working with Prof. McNamara in the Computational Aero Elasticity Lab (Hypersonics). I have packed my bags for the summer and am working at Eglin Air Force base in Destin, Florida for the AFRL on a joint research project.

My life isn't just about airplanes though...I have a passion for entrepreneurship, startups and all things tech. I am highly motivated to translate my passion for creating cool products, meeting new people and changing the world into a business in the future. As of now, I am the President of the Business Builders Club at Ohio State.

When I'm not in the lab, you can catch me golfing, playing tennis, or going for a dive (at least while I'm in Florida..)

Drop me a line. I'd love to chat.
